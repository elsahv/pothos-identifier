About Elsa and this blog

Since graduating with my Bachelor’s Degree in Nutrition and Food Science, my career and life goals have had a few wonderful (and not so wonderful!) plot twists….

It wasn't until graduation when I realized that I wanted to do something different with my life. I know... pretty cliche!!

After some floundering and trying a few different jobs, I've realized how much I wanted to pursue a creative career in front-end development.


One thing that remains unchanged however, is my interest in food chemistry and fitness. Some time has passed... to be honest, I had forgotten a lot of things learned from my classes. I still love nutrition, and have decided to use this blog to supplement my web developers portfolio, and to remember some of those forgotten facts.


    What this blog is not about

  • Small disclaimer: I’m not a dietitian, lifestyle coach, or fitness trainer, I’m not really an advocate of diets either.
  • I’m an aspiring web developer who loves foods equally, whether its healthy/organic, or processed and fattening.
  • My goal with this blog is to research and remember all the fun food facts I’ve learned in college, and to create and retain a healthier, more positive lifestyle.
